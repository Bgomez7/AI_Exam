AI EXAM GENERATOR

Generate exams using large language models (LLMs).

Text or Topic?

The text option allows sections from a textbook to be inputted to generate questions. The topic option can be a general or specific topic. Using the AI option selected, the AI will generate questions over the inputted topic.

Gemini or GPT-3.5?

Both options are considered large language models. Gemini is developed by Google and out-performs GPT-3.5 on many metrics. Gemini is considered to be the better option. GPT-3.5, developed by OpenAI was the first large language model to shock and impress the world.
